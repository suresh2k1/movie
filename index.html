ALLURI SITARAMA RAJU

Alluri Sitarama Raju was born on July 4, 1897. Right from his childhood, he always wanted to fight against the British. He was fondly called Manyam Veerudu, which roughly translated to 'hero of the forest. He headlined the Rampa Rebellion of 1922 against the British Madras Forest Act of 1882. The act restricted the land rights of the tibals.

RRR movie

RRR is directed by SS Rajamouli.

The film is a fictional tale on two Telugu freedom fighters, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem. RRR won a Golden Globe and Oscar for Best Original Song. By Janani K: SS Rajamouli's RRR created history by becoming the first-ever Indian film to win an Oscar for Best Original Song. Over the past year, RRR has been making waves not just in India but also in the international circuit. In fact, the West has lapped up RRR just like one of their own. A year later, RRR now has an Oscar, a Golden Globe and countless recognitions from Hollywood.
